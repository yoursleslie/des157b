How much do you think the average
American's car expenditure is?

$1,025

per month, on average

Source: AAA Your Driving Costs, 2024

The $1,025/month adds up to over $12,000 per year and $180,000 in 15 years For most Americans, this is more than what they spend on food in a year.
However, these car costs do not stay fixed over time as they are directly tied to oil prices. With fuel and depreciation being the two largest slices, they are directly correlated to oil market fluctuations.
Therefore, when oil prices rise, so does your car payment — even if your daily routine does not change.
