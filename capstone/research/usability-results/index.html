Usability Testing Results

Capstone — DES 157B

3 Observations

1. User tended to skip interactive elements

During all 3 tests, users initially skipped the map and pie chart. Instead they went straight to reading and/or skipped the page entirely. Even after exploring the project, some even read past the interactive elements. The interactive data pieces need stronger visual inidcations to help users engage with the site.

2. Buttons don't work or look unfinished

Testers noticed buttons were unstyled and gave no user feedback. Testers often had to double click the button to make it work properly. Every button needs a visible response when clicked and should be fuctional no matter where click is done.

3. The project feels too short/not enough content

All three testers finishedin under three minutes with some saying it felt brief. The timeline was seen as the part to lengethen. Some suggestions include making the timeline horizontal or also interactive.

Reflection

What needs improvement

Button interactivity is the biggest fix at the moment. Styling comes second as also an urgent fix. The closing section also needs to respond to user choices and should have a bigger impact. This section should be more impactful and call the user for action.

Are users able to navigate?

They could scroll through fine, but interactive elements were often missed or ignored. The map and chart both went unnoticed multiple times across many users. Call out, overlays and styling can help further indicate the interactive elements.

Does it land the message?

It is making progress there. Testers found it thought-provoking and liked the user of storytelling. However, the project being short and history heavy makes the impact get lost. The message needs more room/explaining in order to land properly.

What modifications are needed

Style and cleaning up the buttons. Addings callouts to signal interactivity. Make every country a different color and add to descriptions. Replace vocabulary like "expenditure" with plain language. Break up sections to increase length.

  3. Detirmine button styling and fix fuctionality
