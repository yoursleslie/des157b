Tip Caclulator

My Function calculates the total amount you should tip based on your bill subtotal and tip percentage. I got inspiration from going out to eat and figuring out how much I shoudl tip.

Tip Caclulator

            const tipTotal = function(subTotal, tipPercent) {
                return subTotal * (tipPercent / 100);
            }
